Nice Brass is a 11-member brass band based in NYC. We like to play in parks, at your next birthday party, bar or bat mitzvah, pet daycare, or really anything else. We play family‑friendly music including New Orleans standards, pop songs, and (occasionally) the Jonas Brothers — special requests can be negotiated.
